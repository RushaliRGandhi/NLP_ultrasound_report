Rules for the surgicl report  perforated appendicitis grading:


Grade 1 : Localized/Contained perforation

Grade 2 : Contained abscess without peritonitis

Grade 3 : Generalized peritonitis without dominant abscess

Grade 4 : Generalized peritonitis with one/more abscesses

if non of the mentioned condition is met, then it is a simple appendicitis and
    we can assign grade 0 for it.
